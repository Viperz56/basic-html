Subhas Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist whose defiant patriotism made him a hero in India,
			 but whose attempt during World War II to rid India of 
				British rule with the help of Nazi Germany and Imperial Japan left a troubled legacy.
